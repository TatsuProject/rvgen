# Code of conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short:

- Be respectful and inclusive. We welcome contributors regardless of
  experience level, gender, ethnicity, or background.
- Constructive criticism is welcome; personal attacks are not.
- If you witness or experience unacceptable behaviour, contact the
  maintainers privately (see SECURITY / email contact at the bottom of
  [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)).

The full text of the covenant is linked above; it's the authoritative
source. Everyone is expected to follow it in issues, pull requests, and
any community-run spaces (chat, forum, conference talks) that invoke
this project.
